4-Layer Quality Framework

Layer 1: Pre-Production
Domain assessment + calibration before production. First-task pass rate target: ≥80%. Gold standard injection at 5-15% of items.

Domain QA contractor reviews risk-weighted sample. New: 30-50% review. Established: 5-10%. Rework target: <8%.

Composite score: Gold accuracy (40%) + Reviewer (30%) + IAA (15%) + Client feedback (15%). Weekly root cause analysis.

Methods for Small Samples

Multi-Armed Bandit: Dynamic traffic allocation to winners. Faster convergence than fixed 50/50 splits.
Sequential Testing: Analyze after every N conversions. Stop early if clear winner. Saves wasted traffic.
Bayesian A/B: "92% probability A beats B." Act at 90% for reversible decisions, 95% for irreversible. More intuitive at small N.
